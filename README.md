# 🥗 FIT KAKA

FIT KAKA is an AI-powered WhatsApp nutrition assistant that makes food tracking simple, fast, and accessible for everyone.

Instead of manually searching for food items, estimating portion sizes, and logging meals inside complicated apps, users can simply send a message or upload a food photo directly on WhatsApp.

The AI analyzes the meal and instantly provides nutritional insights such as:

* 🔥 Calories
* 🍗 Protein
* 🍞 Carbohydrates
* 🧈 Fats
* 📊 Nutrition & Health Insights

FIT KAKA combines the convenience of WhatsApp with AI-powered nutrition analysis to create a seamless and user-friendly health tracking experience.

# 🎯 Problem FIT KAKA Solves

Most nutrition tracking applications are difficult to use consistently because they require:

* Manual food searches
* Portion size estimation
* Repetitive meal logging
* Switching between multiple screens

FIT KAKA removes this friction by allowing users to track meals naturally through WhatsApp — a platform they already use every day.


# Features

## 📸 AI Food Recognition

* Upload food images directly through WhatsApp
* AI detects food items automatically
* Instant nutritional breakdown

## 💬 WhatsApp-Based Tracking

* No separate app required
* Track meals through simple chat messages
* Fast and effortless logging experience

## 📊 Smart Nutrition Analysis

* Calories tracking
* Protein, carbs, and fats analysis
* Meal history and nutrition insights

## 🎯 Personalized Health Tracking

* Daily nutrition goals
* Health score analysis
* Personalized diet suggestions

## 📈 Dashboard & Analytics

* Track eating habits over time
* View nutrition trends
* Monitor health progress visually

# How to get started ?

1. Visit the website
2. Click the **Get Started** button
3. Open WhatsApp chat with FIT KAKA
4. Send:

   * A food photo 📸
   * Or a text description 📝
5. Receive instant AI-powered nutrition analysis
6. Track your progress from the dashboard
